
PCB constraints:

PCB Track/Spacing: 
    Gatema: > 0.15mm / 0.15mm
    PCBWAY: > 0.153m / 0.153mm (6mil), or: 0.127mm / 0.127mm (5mil)

    ==> use MIN: 0.160mm / 0.160mm in our boards!
    ==> use STD: 0.25mm / 0.20mm

Hole:
    Gatema: > 0.3mm diam.
    PCBWAY: > 0.25mm diam.

    ==> use MIN hole / anular: 0.3mm / 0.46 mm
    ==> use STD hole / anular: 0.4mm / 0.8mm
